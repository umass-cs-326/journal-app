Planned Features

A quick preview of what the Journal App will support as we build it out.

  1. Secure sign-up and sign-in with session-based auth
  2. Creating, editing, and deleting journal entries
  3. Rich text editing with bold/italic and lists
  4. Tagging entries and filtering by tag
  5. Full-text search across all entries
  6. Daily reminders and streak tracking
  7. Autosave with offline support and sync
  8. Export to PDF or Markdown
  9. Dark/light theme toggle with persistence
  10. Share individual entries via secure links
